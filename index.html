RULES

    The game consists of 2 players
    Each player must answer all riddles throughout the map to proceed
    If a player gets an incorrect answer they lose 10HP
    Players can gain health by going through pink health packs
    Once riddles are answered navigate towards the middle
    First player to reach the middle wins

Controls:

    Player 1 movement: W, A, S, D
    Player 1 answer: 1, 2, 3
    Player 2 movement: ARROW KEYS
    Player 2 answer: 7, 8, 9
